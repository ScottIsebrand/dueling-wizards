Dueling Wizards

You and fellow Elementalist wizard Osmanwic are fighting a duel using spells, which of which harnesses the power of one of the Four Elements: Water, Fire, Earth, and Wind. How much a duelist's spell weakens their opponent is affected by the opponent's cast spell, as shown in the Damage Table below. It is customary among Elementatists to contest each other five times per duel, at which point the wizard who has been weakened the most yields. When both have been equally weakened, they agree to call the duel a draw.
